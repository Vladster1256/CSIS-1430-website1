How to Play Super Smash Bros. 4 properly

  1. Make sure you know the controls before you play the game
  2. Begin by playing around with the characters and find one you like
    • After you pick that character, watch some combo and strategy videos
    • Go to practice mode or smash mode and play an easy computer using those strategies in the videos you watched
    • begin to use those combos and moves you seen from the video in real matches and results
  3. Begin to practice counters to situations
    • Practice spot dodges for grabs and faster smash attacks
    • Practice using rolls strategically
    • Practice shielding for the right moves such as smashes and longer attacks
    • Practice shield grabbing for easy wins
    • WHAT EVER YOU DO NEVER LET YOUR SHEILD BREAK
  4. The best game to play is the smart game
    • This includes experimenting carefully to see what weaknesses the oponent has
    • Look for holes in the technique and catch habits
  5. NEVER PLAY WITH ITEMS!!!!
